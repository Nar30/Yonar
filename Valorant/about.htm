About Us

Connect Yonar is dedicated to assisting in both the moderation force and casual players of the Valorant Discord servers. We are a community that brings top-quality, useful resources and a good home for you in finding the right role or simply to enjoy the game more.

Our Mission

  • Facilitate Opportunities: Help individuals search or apply for moderator positions or find enjoyable Valorant communities.
  • Create a community of moderators and players who are going to gather and spend quality time with each other in playing and collaborating with Valorant.

What We Offer?

  • Guidance and Support: Whether you're looking to get into a mod role or you want some tips on how to play better, the team is here for individualized guidance and support.
  • Knowledge Sharing: Let's combine our expertise in moderation and in gaming. Our team is constantly contributing to our guides, resources, and forums in order to make sure you've got the best tools at your disposal.
  • Community Engagement: Join us in a wide range of community activities and discussions. We are here to assist you in feeling comfortable and finding your place in our community, as well as in forming effective relations.
