Events and Tournaments

At Connect Yonar, we believe in the power of community and connection. We host a variety of regular events designed to help you engage with others, whether you’re looking to moderate or simply enjoy the game. Our events provide valuable opportunities for networking, learning, and fun.

Types of Events

  • Moderation Workshops: Attend or participate in some of the planned workshops to hone your moderation skills. Learn from seasoned moderators, discuss best practice, and discover community management at its best.
  • Gameplay Sessions: Get scheduled gameplay sessions where you can team up with other players to collaborate, share strategies, and have some friendly competitive action going on.
  • Q&A Sessions: Listen to live Q&A with Valorant experts and moderators. Get your questions answered and learn from what has been happening to others, too, and find out what's new in the Valorant community.
  • Social Events: Participate in casual social events such as game nights, community challenges, and themed discussions. These are opportunities to meet new people and make lifelong friends.
  • Community Challenges: Take part in community-driven challenges where you can compete, collaborate, and showcase your skills. These challenges are designed to be fun and engaging, bringing members together to achieve common goals.

How to Participate

To get involved in our events, start by checking our events calendar regularly. The calendar is updated with the latest information about each event, including new additions and changes.

Once you've found events that interest you, make sure to register for them. Some events require advance registration to ensure a spot, so being an early bird can be beneficial.

Finally, actively participate by joining the conversation. Engage with other attendees, share your thoughts, ask questions, and make the most out of your experience by being actively involved.

We look forward to seeing you at our events and helping connect you with others in the Valorant community.

Moderation Workshop

Attend, or participate in some of them to fine-tune your moderation skills. Learn from experienced moderators, discuss best practices, and discover community management at its best. Whether you're a new mod or need more practice, workshops offer a deep dive into handling tricky situations, cultivating a healthy community interaction process, and how to strictly enforce rules and guidelines. It will keep you busy in hands-on activities, allow you to explore real-world case studies, and connect you with fellow aspiring moderators in this learning environment.

Gameplay Sessions

Gameplay Sessions Game with other players in our scheduled gameplay sessions collaborate, share tactics and strategies, and engage in some friendly competitive action. Whether you practice new techniques, want to try new roles, or just hang out and enjoy playing the game with others, these sessions are made for you to have some fun and relaxed atmosphere. Team up with other players of all levels, form teams, live through the thrill of tactical gameplay, and form long-lasting connections within the community.

Casual Events

Casual social events, game nights, community challenges, and themed discussions are open for participation. These events help to bond with others in the relaxed atmosphere of sharing passion over gaming, making lifelong friends, and introducing people in games. They include fun competitions with friends, team works on joint activities, and contentious discussions. Join in on and become part of our active and growing community!

Community Challenges

Community Challenges Compete, collaborate, or show off your skills with these community-driven challenges. Engaging, fun, and all-inclusive, the members strive for a common goal that brings the community together-it can be about getting high scores, completing objectives in the game, or just solving creative puzzles together. These events promote teamwork as well as friendly competition in which you earn recognition and therefore contribute to the overall success of the community and have a lot of fun!
