Welcome to Connect Yonar

Your source on the internet for aspiring Discord moderators and casual Valorant players. Want some help in becoming a moderator or looking for a way to improve your gameplay? Not to mention becoming part of a lively Valorant-loving community, you've found the right place in Connect Yonar.

At Connect Yonar, we are looking for those interested in becoming moderators and those just looking to have fun at Valorant. Our website provides:

Key Sections:

  • Moderator Role Applications: To search and apply for moderator positions in Valorant Discord servers.
  • Guides and Resources: It will provide you with guides and resources to improve your moderation and even enhance gameplay.
  • Community Connections: Join the community to connect with fellow Valorant players and moderators. Share experiences and befriend other members.
